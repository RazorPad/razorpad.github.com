RazorPad

The next best thing to hit the web since the awesome Razor View Engine

RazorPad is a online editor to try out the awesome Razor Engine provided by ASP.NET MVC.

Get Started

Copy paste your views/model/controller snippets and hit execute! Or you can start from scratch! If you're feeling developer's block, check out some quick start examples to get inspired

Read more

Get Social

Wrote an awesome snippet that you want to share with the world? Helping out fellows at forums like StackOverflow? Need to embed a snippet on your blog? Need to post it on Social Networks? Simply save your snippet and hit Share! Then copy the link that pops over and paste it anywhere you want. The snippet will live forever. And best of all - will be free forever!

Contribute

Feel like getting your hands dirty with code? How about adding a help page or two? Or improvise the site's design? Razorpad is available on github along with the source for this site! Fork it to get down under and repay your debt to the world!
